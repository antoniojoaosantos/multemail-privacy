
# Privacy Policy – Multemail

The **Multemail** extension respects user privacy and complies with Google Chrome Web Store policies and applicable data protection regulations.

Multemail uses only **official Google APIs**, including the **Gmail API**, to allow users to connect two Gmail accounts and view received emails from both inboxes in a unified interface.

The data accessed by the extension includes:
- the Google account email address (to identify Account A and Account B);
- email metadata (subject, sender, date, and snippet).

This data:
- is used **solely for the functionality of the extension**;
- is **not stored on external servers**;
- remains locally within the user’s browser;
- is **not sold, shared, or transferred to third parties**.

Multemail does **not** use data for advertising, analytics, or marketing purposes.

Users can revoke the extension’s access to their Google account at any time via their Google account security settings.
